07 August 2021

Urumasi updated:

  • Cryogenic storage now applies stasis to stop your metabolism.
  • You no longer snore while in deep cryogenic sleep.

09 July 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • map export now works and can be used to save an area of the map
  • Transit tube dispenser stations are now buildable via RPD
  • Transit tube dispenser station sprite errors have been fixed. The sprites themselves have not been changed, but the names and orientation has been corrected.

01 July 2021

MaltVinegar updated:

  • increases the RCL's capacity to 900

29 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Turbines no longer produce ridiculous power and throw tons of runtimes when the intake is a wall
  • Conveyor switches can be toggled between two-way and one-way with a screwdriver when in item form
  • Conveyor switches remember their one-way state when turned into item form
  • You can now use the Activate Held Object and Drop Object hotkeys (default Z and Q, respectively) to activate arm-implanted tools and put objects away. You must have the hand free before you can activate it this way.
  • Arm implant tools are no longer no-drop, and instead simply snap back if they get dropped. Don't sweat it, if you slip just press Z again.

Urumasi updated:

  • You can now carefully walk over piles of items to avoid tripping over them.
  • We have recalibrated recyclers to stop them from eating your hardsuit helmet lights.
  • Nanotrasen apologizes for the shipment of defective deepcore pinpointers and sent a new one on the house.

28 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Gentle slime crossbreeds can be made with pink extracts. They can be used to emulate luminescent activations of extracts.

24 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • All constructors have a universal right-click "Toggle ore silo" verb, notably the plumbing and light constructors

23 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Shuttle mass calculation

22 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • radial menus now ignore the anchor's current alpha, color and transform.

21 June 2021

Urumasi updated:

  • Ore vein pinpointers now correctly only show ores that are on your z-level.
